Mobile Impact Crushing Plant

Mobile impact crushing plant is Shanghai Zenith developed an efficient construction waste disposal equipment, the mobile impact crusher is broken as a host of mobile crushing equipment, bulk materials can be multi-stage crushing, and according to some of the material Specifications for screening, for mining, building materials, highway, railway, construction waste and other industries a complete crushing and screening operations.

Mobile Impact Crusher features

In operation, more efficient direct and effective, it should be more flexible configuration. Waste and scrap widely used in mining, coal, trash, construction waste recycling, earth works, urban infrastructure, road or construction site operations. Mobile impact crusher is mainly used in metallurgy, chemicals, building materials, utilities and other materials often need to move processing operations, especially for highways, railways, hydropower engineering and other stone work flow, users can process raw materials according to the type, size different materials and finished products required the use of a variety of configurations.

  1. It is easy to adjust the crushing gap, enable to control grain-size and keep good particle shape.
  2. Complete crushing function, high productivity, mechanical wear and high comprehensive benefit.
  3. Easy to adapt and flexible to collocate with other machines.
  4. Reliable performance and convenient maintenance.
  5. Quick road transportation. With support legs on truck, quick to set-up.
