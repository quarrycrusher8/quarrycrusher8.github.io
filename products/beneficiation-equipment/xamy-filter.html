XAMY Series Filter

Our XAMY series filters are mainly used in chemical, pharmaceutical, metallurgy, oil, light industry, ferrous metallurgy, ore flotation, mining and other industries for solid-liquid suspension separation.

XAMY Series Filter features

  1. Simple structure, less cover area.
  2. Higher pressure, lower cake moisture.
  3. High utilization rate of return water.
  4. Bigger filtering capacity.
  5. Easy operating, less failure.
