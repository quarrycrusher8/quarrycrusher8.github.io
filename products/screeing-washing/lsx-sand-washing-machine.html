LSX Series Sand Washing Machine

LSX series screw sand washing machine is designed to bigger capacity and higher cleaning level than traditional washing machines. It sets three functions—washing, dewatering, classifying—as a whole, saving the labor and working site space. Now this series sand washing machine has been widely used in various industries. LSX screw sand washing machine is used to remove the dust in sand. It is widely used for cleaning materials in the following industries: quarry, minerals, building materials, transportation, chemical industry, water conservancy and hydropower, cement mixture station and so on.

Sand Washing Machine features

  1. Simple structure, transmission bearing of impeller depart from water and material.
  2. The loss of fine sand occupy very small proportion.
  3. No quick-wear part. Long service life.
  4. Reasonable structure, easy maintenance. High capacity and cleanliness of sand.
  5. Long service life and no quick-wear part, leading to low investment cost and operation cost.
  6. Transmission bearings of impeller depart from water and material, avoiding being eroded.
  7. High capacity and less loss of fine products. Higher washing-cleanliness than traditional ones.
  8. Set functions as a whole, reducing investment costs of other machines.
