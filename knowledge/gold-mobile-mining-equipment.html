Mobile gold washing plant

Gold ore washing plant is very appropriate for placer gold mining. As we know, placer mining is suitable for processing gold deposits found in alluvial deposits of sand and gravel in modern or ancient stream beds, or occasionally glacial deposits. Commonly, operating circumstances of placer gold mining quarry are not extremely excellent for stationary mining equipment. Even so, Zenith's mobile gold washing plant can eradicate these obstacles of placer gold mining. Units of gold washing plant are mounted on wheeled chassis or skid bases which is usually erected and operated very close towards the mining location. In terms of capacity, our mobile gold washing plants are in a position to deal with among 30 tons and 300 tons of material per hour. Each and every washing plant has 3 proportionated sized sluice boxes utilizing 3 distinct riffle styles to optimize gold recovery in all situations.

Mobile concentration plant

In modern day gold mining sector, mobile concentration plant is normally employed in compact and medium gold quarry. Within the procedure of gold mining, gold, becoming of such high density, when place into motion, it's incredibly challenging to slow down, let alone to stop and capture. Straightforward physics states that once the heavy or dense body is put into movement its kinetic power, once in motion, acts to help keep the physique moving. In that case, conventional concentrator can't logically recover any wonderful percentage of gold. However, Zenith's mobile concentration plants have solved this technical dilemma. Meanwhile, each and every mobile concentration plant adopts the high-quality concentrator, which can boost the mining efficiency substantially.
