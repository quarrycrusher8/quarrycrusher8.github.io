Slate Gravel And Powder Crusher Pulverizer

While slate stone is a kind hard, abrasive metamorphic rock. Shanghai Zenith Company will supply jaw crusher, cone crusher for the slate crushing. Complete set of stationary, portable, mobile crushing plant for slate is also available for the clients.

slate powder processing line equipment

Slate crushing and screening plant is widely used to process slate rock from quarry deposits for construction application.

Slate crushing and screening plant is widely used for crushing the scrap slate rock for pave and construction application. Zenith Machinery will supply proved heavy duty crushing equipment to crush the hard, abrasive slate stone. The vibrating screen is also widely used in the slate extraction for sieving.

In the slate stone quarrying and processing plant, compact jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, vertical shaft impact crusher equip with diesel engine is widely used for stone crushing.

The compact crushing equipment can save the foot space, enable operation in constrained environments, they also can produce products in consistently size.

The simplified flow chart of slate crushing and screening plant is: vibrating feeder -> PE jaw crusher -> Impact crusher (or cone crusher, VSI crusher) -> vibrating screen -> stockpile.

The final grain sizes of slate crushing plant are: 0-2mm, 2-4mm, 4-8mm,8-16mm, 16-30mm, 30-45mm, 45-64mm and larger. According to the application, we design the proved river stone crushing & screening plant of 40-80 TPH, 80-120 TPH, 120- 200TPH, 200-300TPH, 300-450TPH, 450-600TPH, 600-800TPH, 800-1000 TPH etc. for the contractors and clients.
