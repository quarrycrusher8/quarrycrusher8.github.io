Silica Sand Washing Plant

The cost of washing is the most simple method and minimum clean silica sand. In some very pure mineral resources, there are a lot of minerals, lots of clay and mud, and no surface dyeing. Washing is enough to produce acceptable grade products.

sand washing plant introduction

Our sand washing system has been used all over the world to replace traditional, inefficient sand washing systems, such as bucket wheels and sand screw / screw classifiers. These washing systems are used to produce special sand, such as quartz sand, golf sand and filter sand, for urban sewage treatment.

advantages

  1. High efficiency, high output and little sand loss during washing
  2. reasonable structure, excellent performance. The transmission device isolates with sand and water, which greatly reduces the failure rate.

working principle of sand washer

After slowing down, the motor runs slowly by belt, reducer and gear wheel, replacing it. Sand and stone are washed and washed and washed, and replaced, and grinded each other. The impurities that cover the sand are being washed away. At the same time, adding more water, the powerful water will take away the waste and some low waste cleaning and washing. Through the above process, the washing function is completed. Clean sand and stone are lifted up from the blade. As it slowly rises, the water begins to fall through the grid and returns to wash the washing. On the one hand, water is removed from sand and stone; on the other hand, water can be made full use of. Finally, the sand and stone are rotated and replaced to the outside washing task.
