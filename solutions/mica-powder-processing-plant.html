Mica Powder Processing Plant

Mica is one of the most difficult minerals to grind. Some of the same properties that make mica useful make it difficult to grind. Even thin flakes are though and elasitc and resist abrasion and reduction in size by the conventional types of grinding equipment.In the wet ground mica powder processing plant, the mica material from the crushing and screening operations is subject to coarse and fine grinding primarily in roller mills or ball mills to reduce the material to the necessary product size range.

Two processes, dry grinding and wet grinding, are employed to produce ground mica. The products of the two processes differ from each other in certain physical properties. Most plants which beneficiate mica have dry-grinding equipment as an integral part of the plant.

Wet Ground Mica Powder Application

Many foreign factories have applied wet ground mica powder in the rubber tyres, it can effectively avoid inner tyre adhering to the outer tyre, and meanwhile wet ground mica powder is used as demold material for making rubber products. Wet ground mica powder used in cables can increase cable isolationand avoid wrapper over vulcanization and cable core adhesion.

Mica powder in various forms is obtained by grinding/breaking mica scrap, which is a slow, costly and extremely complicated process, because of the scrap being tough and having a plate like structure.
