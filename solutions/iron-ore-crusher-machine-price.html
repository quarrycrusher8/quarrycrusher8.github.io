Processing iron ore is a tough duty for any type of crusher, however, Zenith impact crusher is a model which is especially designed for such applications. Portable Iron Ore Crushing Plant

Liberation is the first step in iron ore processing and consists mostly of crushing and grinding. The iron ore must be crushed to a particle size sufficiently close to the grain size of the iron-bearing mineral to allow for a high degree of mineral liberation.

One or two stages of crushing may be performed at the mine prior to shipping the raw material to the processing facility. Gyratory crushers are generally used for primary crushing, and cone crushers are used for secondary and tertiary fine crushing. Vibrating screens remove undersize material from the feed to the next crusher and allow for closed-circuit operation of the fine crushers. All the iron crushing plant can be in portable and mobile application.
