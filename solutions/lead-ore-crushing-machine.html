Lead Ore Crushing Machine

Lead ore crushing machine is also used as the primary crusher for crushing gold ore in gold ore crushing industry. Jaw crusher is the most used gold mining machine for crushing ore. Jaw crusher can reach the crushing ratio of 4-6 and the shape of final product is even. So gold jaw curshers are widely applied to crush high hardness, mid hardness and soft rocks and ores such as slags, construction materials, marble，etc.

Lead ore crushing machine processing line

Lead ore crushing machine materials are given towards the jaw crusher (primary gold ore crusher) evenly and steadily by vibrating feeder by way of a hopper for primary crushing. After first crush, the pad will gone to live in impact crusher by belt conveyor for secondary crush the crushed materials will gone to live in vibrating screen for separating. After separating, the many components that may meet standard is going to be moved away as final items, because the other locations is going to be returned to affect crusher, thus developing a closed circuit. Size final items could possibly be combined and rated based on customer’s specific requirement. We could also equip dust catcher system to guard atmosphere.
