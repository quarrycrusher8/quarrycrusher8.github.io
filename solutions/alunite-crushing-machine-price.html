Alunite Crushing Machine Price

Alunite crushing and grinding process are very important steps in alunite powder production line. In terms of alunite crushing machine, we have jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher for you. Among them, jaw crusher are used for primary crushing, and impact crusher or cone crusher are used for secondary or fine crushing. Both of them are of high efficiency and low operating cost in the corresponding stage.

Alunite introduction

Alunite occurs as veins and replacement masses in trachyte, rhyolite, and similar potassium rich volcanic rocks. It is formed by the action of sulfuric acid bearing solutions on these rocks during the oxidation and leaching of metal sulfide deposits. Alunite also is found near volcanic fumaroles. The white, finely granular masses closely resemble finely granular limestone, alunite, anhydrite, and magnesite in appearance. The more compact kinds from Hungary are so hard and tough that they have been used for millstones.

Alunite crushing process

Raw alunite stone are evenly fed into the jaw crusher by vibrating feeder for primary crushing. Then the belt conveyor transfers the elementary products into impact crusher for secondary crushing. Then, the secondary products are separated into different sizes by the vibrating screen, and the alunite particles which do not reach the required size will be return to the impact crusher for re-crushing.

Alunite stone crushing process includes vibrating feeder, jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, vibrating screen, belt conveyor, electric controlling system and dust catcher etc. The designed capacity is from 50t/h to 500t/h. To meet customer's specific requirements, we can make different design to meet various needs.

Alunite crushing machine

A complete alunite crushing production line contains the following parts: vibrating feeder, primary crushing equipment (Jaw Crusher), secondary crushing equipment (Cone Crusher or Impact Crusher), Vibrating Screen, vibrating feeder, sand washer, mobile belt conveyor, electric control panel and so on. These alunite crushing machines can efficiently improve the productivity.

Besides independent crushers, alunite crushing plant are also widely used by quarry operators. It has the following characteristics: with a high degree of automation, low cost, big crushing ratio, energy saving, high yield, less pollution, convenient maintenance and so on.
