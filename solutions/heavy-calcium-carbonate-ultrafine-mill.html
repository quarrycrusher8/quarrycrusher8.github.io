Investigation On The Blockage Of The Duct Of Ultrafine Mill

For ultrafine mill production process, will be affected by many factors, may produce more problems, one of the common faults is the phenomenon of duct jam, this phenomenon, will cause equipment efficiency drop, finished product quality unreasonable phenomena such as the production, because the wind in the air duct size is the main factor affecting the fineness of the finished product, If the air duct jam, then the wind flow is not smooth, can not be ground after the material away, screening and other operations, so the efficiency will drop, the quality of the finished material is unreasonable, in view of this situation, we need to troubleshoot this problem.

First check the feed situation, in general, considering the ultrafine mill processing capacity, in the feed, we can not appear in the feed too fast or uneven situation, so that the finished materials can not be in a timely manner under the role of the blower exhaust from the air duct, resulting in duct congestion, requirements in the feeding time, to achieve continuous, uniform;

Second, check whether the dust remover is normal, if the dust remover can not be normal operation, will cause a large number of dust particles in the wind tunnel accumulation, so that the equipment of the air duct congestion, so to regularly check whether the dust can be normal operation, problems to be resolved in a timely manner;

Again, check the blower power adequacy, if not enough, the material in the air duct there is no way to flow normally, the time will accumulate in a piece, which is also an important reason for the ultra-fine mill duct jam, in addition, the lack of voltage will also cause the blower wind can not carry out the movement of material transmission, Check the equipment's rated power and operating voltage as normal during overhaul
