According to closing solutions purposes, stone crushing is especially operated in a few stages, primary crushing, secondary crushing and tertiary crushing. Jaw, impactor, or gyratory crushers are generally used for initial reduction. The crusher item, normally 7.five to 30 centimeters in diameter, and also the grizzly throughs are discharged on to a belt conveyor and typically are conveyed to the surge pile for short term storage or are bought as coarse aggregates.

Tertiary crushing is often carried out using cone crushers or other kinds of impactor crushers. Oversize product through the best deck of the sizing screen is fed on the tertiary crusher.
