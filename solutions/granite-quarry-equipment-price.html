Granite Quarry Equipment Price

Commercial quarry production of natural stone is popular in most countries of the world, such as Italy, Spain, France, Germany, Greece, Norway, Brazil, Ukraine etc. Technology for stone extraction differs from other solid minerals deposits mining technologies.

In granite quarrying opeartion, a deep hole is bored by drilling method in a rock, where the charge is laid with following blasting. Among the breakaway pieces of rock the largest boulders are selected, which are then sawn into slabs.Drilling and blasting is done with heavy duty down the hole and top hammer drill rigs to a preset pattern. Once the bench is cut or split loose from the deposit, heavy equipment is used to lift the granite bench and transfer it to further quarrying equipment.
