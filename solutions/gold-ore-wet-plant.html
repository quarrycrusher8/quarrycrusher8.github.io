Gold ore Grinding Plant for Sale

The benefit of fine grinding gold ores is to liberate the fine gold particles and recover them in a inexpensive gravity concentrator, like the Falcon Concentrator, with a bowl specifically designed for fines recovery. Gravity concentration is usually the least expensive method to recover ore. This works great with oxide ores, but on sulfide ores, the ultrafine mill has a benefit, also. One can fine grind the ore, either liberating the gold (in the case of pyritic gold ores where the gold has a fine coating of iron, or simply concentrate the gold bearing sulfides into a "Super Concentrate", with a high percentage of values, to be chemically processed further to liberate the gold. This method greatly reduces the quantity of concentrate which has to be treated chemically, or by other methods, which in turn reduces the cost of treating the ores considerably.

Gold ore mining processing

Gold ore mining processing generally requires several steps, the first is mine blasting, then big ore is transported to the crusher for crushing , crushing granularity controlled by screening equipment to meet the requirements of the minerals,qualified gold ore are transported to a ball mill, through Hydrocyclone unit meet the requirements of the current mineral timely elected, avoid excessive grinding, mineral do not meet the requirements return ball mill for secondary grinding. When less than 60%-80% mineral accounting purposes 200, to carry out mineral sorting operations. Gold benefication method is generally used flotation cell or leaching equipment.
